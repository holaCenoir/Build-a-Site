Cheese

There are many types of Cheese. Each one of them have their own unique taste. We will be going over many famous types of cheese and giving you a discription of their taste.

American cheese has a mild flavor and smooth texture. Its color may range from white to light yellow or orange. It's typically sold in the form of solid blocks, shredded, cubes, spread, or individually wrapped slices commonly known as “singles.” This is a very common cheese around America and is well known to be put on burgers. This cheese is a delight.

Mozzarella is made fresh daily in many parts of Italy and in Italian cheese shops within the United States and elsewhere. Creamy white or pale ivory in colour, mozzarella has a mild, fresh lactic flavour and is used primarily in cooking, especially as a main ingredient in pizza.

Swiss cheese is a cool and hole-y cheese that you might recognize by its fun bubbles and pale yellow color. It comes from Switzerland and is made from cow's milk, giving it a smooth and kind of creamy feel. Whether you're putting it in a sandwich or melting it in a tasty dish, Swiss cheese adds a delicious and nutty flavor that makes your food extra awesome!
